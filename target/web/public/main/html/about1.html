JEMTEC



JIMS Engineering Management Technical Campus is approved by AICTE & Affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), Delhi.



The Institute has been established to cater to the growing needs of higher education in the country. In order to face the challenges of globalization and liberalization in the field of engineering, the Institute has endowed its faculty and students with modern technologies, skills and attitudes.

The Institute has an excellent infrastructure in support of high quality teaching, learning and research activities. The Wi-Fi enabled campus has the state-of-art infrastructure comprising of an environment friendly Administrative Block, Academic Blocks, spacious class rooms with internet and intranet connectivity and hi-tech multimedia and laboratories for each stream of Engineering and Technology.
